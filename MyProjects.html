Paper Trading Copycat

I am currently working on an application that copies and executes specific trades from the top 10-20 traders on paper trading platforms. It will be delayed, of course, but it should seek to replicate the returns and actions of the top traders. it will screen only reasonable trades, ignoring massive put or call orders on thinly traded stocks. It is currently being developed using python.
